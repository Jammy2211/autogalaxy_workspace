The ``results/workflow`` folder contains example scripts for creating a workflow via the results of a **PyAutoGalaxy** model-fit to inspect and interpret results quickly.

Files
-----

- ``csv_maker``: Make .csv files from the modeling results which summarize the results of a large samples of fits.
- ``png_maker``: Make .png files of every fit, to quickly check the quality of the fit and interpret the results.
- ``fits_maker``: Make .fits files of every fit, to quickly check the quality of the fit and interpret the results.
